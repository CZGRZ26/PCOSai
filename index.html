Why PCOSai

Not another
wellness influencer.

PCOSai was built because the information gap after a PCOS diagnosis is real and harmful. Most people leave their doctor's office with a prescription and a vague instruction to "lose weight" — and end up on TikTok.

We built the alternative.

  • 📚

    Grounded in clinical guidelines

    Every response is anchored in the 2023 International PCOS Clinical Guidelines — the gold standard used by specialists worldwide.

  • 🚫

    No shame. No fad diets.

    PCOSai never suggests restrictive diets, pushes weight loss as a cure, or sells supplements. That's not evidence-based care.

  • 🩺

    Helps you work with your doctor

    The goal is to make your medical appointments more effective — not to replace them. PCOSai helps you advocate for yourself.

  • 💛

    Designed for the whole person

    PCOS affects mental health, body image, relationships, and work. PCOSai treats it that way.
